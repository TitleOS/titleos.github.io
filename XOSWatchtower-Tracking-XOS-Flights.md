Today I launched [https://xoswatchtower.titleos.dev/](https://xoswatchtower.titleos.dev/), a website that displays and keeps track of the current build flight of Xbox Operating System to various internal and external rings. If you happen to know System Update GUIDs for further flights or rings that aren't listed, feel free to drop me a message or email and I will happily add it to the database. Updates are checked for once a day.
